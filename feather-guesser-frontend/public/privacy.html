Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 2025

Privacy Summary

Feather Guess does not collect or store any personal information from users. We are committed to protecting your privacy while providing an educational and entertaining bird identification experience.

Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Personal identification information (names, email addresses, phone numbers)
  • Location data or GPS coordinates
  • User accounts or login credentials
  • Game scores or personal progress (stored locally only)
  • Device identifiers or tracking information

Third-Party Services

Feather Guess may use third-party advertising services such as Google AdSense to display advertisements. These services may use cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Serve personalized advertisements based on your browsing history
  • Measure advertisement performance and effectiveness
  • Provide relevant content and improve user experience

Cookies and Local Storage

Our website uses minimal local storage to:

  • Remember your game settings and preferences
  • Store your current game progress (locally on your device only)
  • Improve website functionality and user experience

This information never leaves your device and is not transmitted to our servers.

Opting Out of Personalized Advertising

You can opt out of personalized advertising by:

  • Visiting Google Ads Settings
  • Using browser settings to block or delete cookies
  • Enabling "Do Not Track" in your browser preferences
  • Using ad-blocking browser extensions

Children's Privacy

Feather Guess is suitable for all ages and does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Our educational content is designed to be safe and appropriate for young learners interested in birds and nature.

Data Security

Since we do not collect personal information, there is minimal risk to your privacy. All game data is stored locally on your device and is never transmitted to external servers.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or applicable laws. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

Contact Us
